Summer 2025 Online Courses

Hello, I am Dr. Naeemul Hassan, an assistant professor at the University of Maryland at College Park. I am offering two online courses this Summer 2025: Introduction to Python Programming and Introduction to Data Science.

Please indicate your interest by May 25, 2025 using the sign-up link below.

1. Introduction to Python Programming

Concepts Covered:

  • Concept of Variables, Data Types
  • Data Structures [List, Dictionary, Set]
  • Conditional Statements
  • Loop
  • Function
  • Introduction to Pandas
  • Exploratory Data Analysis in Pandas
  • Data Visualization
  • Basic Natural Language Processing using Spacy
  • Capstone Project and Review

Logistics:

  • Duration: 2.5 months
  • Frequency: 1.5 hours per week (Total 15 hours)
  • Format: Online via Zoom
  • Recordings: Sessions will be recorded and available later.

Course Fees:

Think about it as an investment you are doing on yourself to develop your skills. Costs cover online meeting arrangements, recording storage, and materials preparation.

  • From Bangladesh: 10,000 Taka (Bkash)
  • From USA: 100 USD (Paypal, Zelle, etc.)

Schedule:

  • Tentative: June - August 2025
  • Specific days/times will be decided based on the availability of the majority of interested participants.
